Transforming Lives Since 2015

For nearly a decade, Charity For All Uganda has been at the forefront of community development, working hand-in-hand with local leaders to create sustainable change. Our holistic approach addresses immediate needs while building infrastructure for future generations.

  • Partnered with 50+ local communities
  • Trained 200+ local volunteers
  • Established 10 community centers
  • Provided medical care to 5,000+ individuals
  • Supported education for 800+ children
